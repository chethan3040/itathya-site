Questions

Honest answers.

Is it really free?+

Yes. No card, no trial timer, no surprise paywall. We may add an optional pro tier later, but the core — Pomodoro, homework, AI coach, streaks, achievements — stays free forever.

What's "Ember"?+

Ember is the AI coach. It learns when you actually focus, what subjects drain you, and slots tasks into your peak windows. Less generic productivity advice, more "you ship 88% of your hard work before noon — let's protect that."

Will it work without an account?+

You can try the timer in guest mode. To save streaks, sync homework, or earn XP, you'll want to sign up — takes about 30 seconds.

Does it integrate with Google Classroom?+

Paste a syllabus, photograph an assignment, or import a Google Doc — Focus Forge auto-tags by subject and estimates time. Direct Google Classroom sync is on the roadmap for this term.

Who's behind it?+

Focus Forge is a product by itathya — a small studio building tools for people who want to take their work seriously. Reach us at support@itathya.com.
